About Student Job Portal

A dedicated career placement and internship network built entirely on Django, designed to empower academic campuses and student bodies.

Our goal is to narrow the divide between employers and entry-level college students. We realize that traditional job boards can feel overwhelming and are often filled with requirements that students cannot meet. Student Job Portal provides a curated, vetted environment where students can secure roles aligned with their specific majors and graduation deadlines.

Every posting undergoes a comprehensive administrative check before becoming searchable. This vetting step ensures legitimacy and safeguards our student pool against fraudulent postings.

Vetted Security

Admin approvals keep spam out, protecting student credentials and offering secure, reliable listings.

Instant Apply

Students apply in one tap using stored resume assets and tailored cover letters.

Personalized Lists

Keep track of interesting positions using custom bookmarks and follow application updates in real time.
